About

KSHITIJ SONI

I'm a vehicle dynamics simulation engineer who lives at the intersection of multi-body modelling, control systems, and real-world validation. From tire dynamics on a Formula Student car to full battery-electric commercial vehicle platforms, I care about how vehicles actually behave on the road.

I hold a European dual-degree from Czech Technical University in Prague and HAN University of Applied Sciences, and I'm comfortable leading cross-functional teams from concept through prototype validation.
